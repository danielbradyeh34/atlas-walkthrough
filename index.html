Meet ATLAS
A self-sustaining system that sources, enriches, allocates, and sequences leads for our outbound SDR team, with zero manual list building.
Click anywhere or press the right arrow key to walk through each stage of the system.
1 Signal Sources
Where it all starts
Instead of manually pulling lists from ZoomInfo and hoping for the best, ATLAS continuously monitors 11 signal sources for buying intent and prospecting triggers.
Hot signals like pricing page visits, 6sense intent data, and former EH admins who moved to new companies get prioritised. Warm signals like job changes and hiring surges indicate timing. Cold sources fill the pipeline with volume.
The goal: never miss a moment when someone is actively looking for an HR and payroll platform.
2 Enrichment Engine
Filter, enrich, and score
Raw signals are messy. The Enrichment Engine runs every lead through an 8-step qualification process before anything reaches an SDR.
It deduplicates, checks for existing customers and do-not-contact lists, then cascades through ZoomInfo, Cognism, and Lusha to fill in missing data. Every email gets verified. Every lead gets a personalised score based on our custom model.
2,147 raw signals go in. 814 enriched, qualified leads come out. The rest are filtered, non-ICP, or non-compliant.
3 Allocation Engine
The brain that decides
This is where intelligence meets distribution. The Allocation Engine looks at every qualified lead and decides which SDR should get it, and why.
It checks territory rules (Core: 10-150 employees, Mid-Market: 151-1500), enforces ROE compliance, balances workload via round-robin, and ranks by opportunity score. Most importantly, it builds a signal context card explaining exactly why we're reaching out to this person.
SDRs don't just get a name and number. They get the story.
4 Sequencing Engine
Auto-enrolled, ready to go
Once allocated, leads are automatically enrolled into multi-channel outreach sequences. When an SDR logs in, their calls are already loaded and their emails are already queued.
The sequence runs across email, phone, and LinkedIn over 10 days. The first email is AI-generated, referencing the specific signal that triggered the outreach. An AI Reply Handler runs 24/7, classifying responses and auto-booking meetings when prospects say yes.
The SDR's job becomes executing, not sourcing.
5 Pipeline Engine
On-demand capacity
Each SDR has a target contact count (e.g., 200/month). When they need more leads, they hit Request Top-Up and the Pipeline Engine delivers a fresh batch from the qualified pool.
The system validates every request before releasing leads — checking active sequences, conversion rate, and whether existing contacts have been properly worked. No gaming. No churn-and-refresh. When a meeting is booked, the lead is handed off to an AE.
SDRs stay in control. The system stays honest.
6 Feedback Loop
The system that learns
What makes ATLAS a system, not just a pipeline, is the feedback loop. Every outcome feeds back into the machine to make it smarter.
⚡ Auto Top-Up SDR dips below threshold? Pipeline auto-replenishes. ⏰ Stale Rotation No engagement? Remove, free capacity for fresh leads. ♻ 6-Month Recycle Non-converts cool off, then re-enter as warm signals. 🔗 Signal Attribution Which signal actually closed? Reweight the scoring model.
Over time, the system converges on the signals and sequences that actually produce revenue.
✓ Full System
The complete picture
This is ATLAS end-to-end. Signals flow in from 11 sources, get filtered and enriched, allocated to the right SDR, auto-sequenced across channels, and managed by a self-sustaining pipeline engine.
The feedback loop closes the circle: every outcome teaches the system which signals, sequences, and allocations actually produce revenue. The machine gets smarter every week.
Zero manual list building. Zero lead complaints. Just qualified, scored, sequenced contacts landing in your SDRs' queues every day.
