Risks
Execution · Mitigations12 / 14
Known risks and how we stop them

What could kill this,
and how we stop it.

🚫
Prospects hang up on "an AI"
Mitigation: Warm voice, friendly upfront disclosure, ruthless iteration on the first 10 seconds. The opener is everything.
⚠️
Agent qualifies badly, AEs lose trust
Mitigation: Human review of every AI-booked meeting for the first 2 weeks. Hard acceptance threshold before autoloading to AE calendars.
👥
Political blowback from SDR team
Mitigation: Framed as "gives you your weekends back," not "replaces you." SDR leadership involved in playbook design from day one.
⚖️
Compliance incident (AU privacy, AI disclosure)
Mitigation: Legal sign-off before any live call. Recorded disclosure in every opener. Full transcript retention.
🔒
Vendor lock-in / fragility
Mitigation: Qualification logic lives in Relevance AI, not buried in prompts. Voice layer is swap-able.
📭
Dropped leads
Mitigation: Hard fallback. Any call the AI can't complete drops into the morning SDR queue with a flag. No orphan leads, ever.
